Jaylen's Biography

Jaylen Wyatt is a 27-year-old music producer from Missouri City Texas. He currently resides in the city of Houston Texas where he owns a music studio. Jaylen choose to become a music producer at a young age. He grew up around many other around of capitals of the music world but choose to start producing his own music when he did not agree with the music that was being put out at the time. This is where he had turned to learning how to produce sounds on his own from a software at home, where he later pursued a degree in music from the University of Houston. If Jaylen had the chance to do anything else in life, he would like to become a software developer. Jaylen enjoys spending time programming things on the computer and watching it come to life as it develops. He enjoys learning the languages and write ups of the coding world, but also shared that he has a form of color blindness called Tritanopia. This is a condition where a person cannot distinguish between blue and yellow colors. Jaylen says one of his goals is to create a more tailored program towards those with the same condition. One day he also wishes to merge his current hobby and profession of music production and programming to help promote both two together along with creating his own car rental business. Jaylen has several hobbies of his own of course aside from music and software development. In his spare time, he likes to play airsoft, and he also enjoys playing a space game Called Star Citizen where he gets to escape current reality for a while.
